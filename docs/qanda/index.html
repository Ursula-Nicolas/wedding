Information

The venue: Hofgut Heidesheim

We will celebrate at the venue Hofgut Heidesheim, located in the beautiful wine region “Rheinland-Pfalz”, see map at the end of the page.

Address: Heidesheimer Hauptstraße 10, 67283 Obrigheim (Rheinland-Pfalz), Germany
https://hofgut-heidesheim.de/

Day and time

We will celebrate on Saturday, the 20th of November 2021. Start will be at 2 pm. To be able to spend as much time as possible with you, we are also organizing an easy Sunday breakfast at the venue! If you are joining us, please let us know via the (RSVP).

An en detail time table can be found here: (Schedule).

Public transport and car rental

  • Closest airport: International Airport Frankfurt, car rental here
  • Closest main train station: Hauptbahnhof (HBF) Mannheim (tickets here), car rental at Sixt or at Europcar
  • Regional public transport: train station Grünstadt (~4km to the venue)
  • Taxi: http://www.pfalzcar.de/
